About Me

Hi, I'm Sam, a yogini living in Durham North Carolina. I love practicing yoga, surfing, exploring new places, and cooking delicious food, especially when any of the above are spent in the company of good friends. I started doing yoga in 2008, but really fell in love with the practice in 2012 when I discovered alignment-based yoga. In 2016, I completed my RYT-200 yoga teacher training with Sommer and Paul Sobin.

In the whirlwind of ups and downs in graduate school, I was looking for something more than school to keep pushing me outside my comfort zone and to hold me accountable for doing the things that I kept putting off. The yoga practice drew me in, making me push myself, but also bringing me joys in ways I find hard to describe with words. What began as a challenge to myself to learn the names of some of the poses in Sanskrit, turned into an immersion in the yoga philosophy, which lead to yoga teacher training. I have been teaching yoga for the past three years at Duke and local studios and have found an incredible community of students who love the yoga practice as much as I do.

Outside of yoga, I have a Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering from Duke University and I started my own medical device / imaging consulting company, Archer Woods Consulting, LLC.
